Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Clario AI ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Clario AI mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using Clario AI, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you have any questions, contact us at magicjames9star@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Text you submit: When you paste or scan text into Clario, it is sent to OpenAI's API to generate your analysis. We do not store this text on our servers after processing.
  • Saved sessions: If you choose to save a result, it is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • Subscription information: We use Apple's StoreKit to handle all purchases. We do not collect or store payment information directly. Apple processes all transactions.
  • Usage data: We track how many analyses you perform per day (stored locally on your device) to enforce the free tier limit of 3 per day.
  • Camera access: If you use the text scanner, your device camera is used solely to recognize text in real time. No images or video are stored or transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the app — analyzing your text using AI
  • To manage your subscription status via Apple's StoreKit
  • To enforce usage limits on the free tier
  • To improve the app and fix issues

3. Third-Party Services

Clario AI uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Your submitted text is sent to OpenAI to generate analyses. OpenAI's use of data is governed by their Privacy Policy. OpenAI does not use API-submitted data to train their models by default.
  • Apple StoreKit: All in-app purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple. Apple's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.
  • Apple VisionKit: The text scanner uses Apple's on-device VisionKit framework. Text recognition happens entirely on your device and no data is sent to Apple or us.

4. Data Storage and Security

Saved sessions are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. We do not operate any servers that store your personal content. Text submitted for analysis is transmitted securely over HTTPS to OpenAI's API and is not retained by us after the response is returned.

5. Children's Privacy

Clario AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at magicjames9star@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal content on our servers, there is no personal data to request, correct, or delete from our end. All locally saved data can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us
